# Word Echo | Fridge Combo #80

A minimalist vocabulary visualizer and frequency counter designed for writers, editors, and SEO specialists. Instantly see the "echoes" in your text and refine your writing with precision.

## Core Philosophy
- **1 Tool / 1 Action**: Paste your text and see your word frequency ranking instantly—no "Calculate" button required.
- **Identify Overuse**: Visualize which words dominate your writing to avoid repetitive phrasing and improve flow.
- **Seamless Interaction**: Built for the modern workflow with real-time analysis and "Tap to Copy" functionality.

## Key Features
- **Real-time Vocabulary Analysis**: Debounced processing ensures a smooth, lag-free experience even with longer texts.
- **Stop Words Toggle**: Effortlessly filter out common filler words (the, is, at, etc.) to reveal the meaningful keywords in your content.
- **Dynamic Stats Bar**: Track your total word count, character count, and "Uniqueness" (vocabulary diversity) at a glance.
- **Top Rank Highlighting**: The most frequent words are visually emphasized to grab your attention immediately.
- **Tap to Copy**: Found a word you want to reuse or research? Simply tap it in the list to copy it to your clipboard.

## How to Use
1. **Paste Text**: Throw your draft into the textarea.
2. **Review Stats**: Check the "Uniqueness" percentage to see how diverse your vocabulary is.
3. **Filter**: Use the toggle to ignore common words for a deeper analysis.
4. **Action**: Tap on any word in the ranking list to copy it instantly.

## Design System
- **Background**: #fff
- **Main Text**: #333
- **Contrast**: High-visibility borders (#ccc) and subtle data bars for professional utility.
- **Border Radius**: 15px (All elements).
- **Mobile Optimized**: Featuring a centered "Copied!" notification and touch-friendly list items.

## Privacy & Policy
- **No Login**
- **No Tracking**
- **No Ads**
- **Browser Only**: All analysis happens locally on your device. Your text never leaves your browser.
